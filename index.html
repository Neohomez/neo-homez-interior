Ask Us Anything
For a 3BHK apartment in Bangalore, a full home interior typically ranges from ₹8 Lakhs to ₹25 Lakhs depending on the size, material grade, and design complexity. Our Essential package starts at ₹1,200/sq ft and our Premium package at ₹1,800/sq ft. We provide a detailed, transparent quote after the free site visit — no hidden costs.
Typically 30–60 days for a full home interior depending on the scope of work. A single room like a modular kitchen takes 15–20 days. We commit to a fixed timeline upfront and ensure on-time delivery — that's our guarantee to every Neo Homez client.
Yes! Every project includes a detailed 3D design walkthrough before any work begins. You will see exactly how your home will look — room by room — and can request changes before we start execution. This is included free in all our packages.
We serve all areas across Bangalore including Whitefield, Koramangala, HSR Layout, Indiranagar, Jayanagar, JP Nagar, Sarjapur Road, Electronic City, Hebbal, Yelahanka, Bannerghatta Road, and all surrounding areas. We cover the entire Bangalore Urban and Rural district.
All Neo Homez projects come with a minimum 1-year comprehensive warranty covering workmanship, hardware, and finishing. Our Luxury package includes a 2-year warranty. If anything needs attention after handover, our service team responds within 48 hours — at no extra cost.
Absolutely. You can browse our portfolio on this website and on our Instagram @neohomezinterior. We also arrange site visits to completed projects (with client permission) so you can experience the quality firsthand before making any decisions.
We are a full-service interior design and execution studio. We handle everything — design, material sourcing, civil work, carpentry, painting, electrical, plumbing coordination, and final styling. You get a single point of contact and a completely finished home, hassle-free.
