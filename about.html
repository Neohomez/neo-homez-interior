A Message from Founder

Every project at Neo Homez begins with a simple question: how does this family actually live? Not how homes look in magazines — but how mornings unfold in this kitchen, how evenings happen in this living room, and what storage this family will actually use five years from now.

Touheed Ahmed founded Neo Homez Interior with a conviction that Bangalore homeowners deserved better — better transparency, better materials, and better project management — from their interior designers. Too many clients had experienced cost surprises, missed deadlines, and designs that looked nothing like the renders they approved.

With over 10+ years of experience across 15+ residential and commercial projects in Bangalore, Touheed has led interior transformations from 1BHK apartments in Indiranagar to 5BHK villas on Sarjapur Road. His design philosophy balances contemporary aesthetics with practical functionality — spaces that are beautiful to look at and genuinely easy to live in.

Every Neo Homez project is personally overseen by Touheed, not delegated to a junior designer. That personal accountability is why our clients refer us to their neighbours, colleagues, and family.

Design philosophy

"A home should feel like it was made for the person who lives there — not for a design portfolio. My job is to listen carefully enough to understand who you are, and then design a space that reflects that completely."
